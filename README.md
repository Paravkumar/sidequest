# SideQuest

**A campus‑only marketplace where students post and complete micro‑tasks for cash or loot.**

[![Live](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live-sidequest.engineer-0b1020?style=for-the-badge&logo=vercel)](https://sidequest.engineer)
[![Next.js](https://img.shields.io/badge/Next.js-16.1-black?style=for-the-badge&logo=next.js)](https://nextjs.org/)
[![MongoDB](https://img.shields.io/badge/MongoDB-Atlas-13aa52?style=for-the-badge&logo=mongodb)](https://www.mongodb.com/)

SideQuest is a hyper‑local, verified community where students can post “quests” (errands, assignments, tutoring) and earn instantly. The platform is designed for trust, safety, and speed inside each campus.

**Live:** https://sidequest.engineer

---

## Why SideQuest
- **Campus‑locked access** — only verified students can join.
- **Real‑world earning** — quick tasks become instant rewards.
- **Privacy‑first** — contact details are shared only after acceptance.
- **Real‑time chat** — fast coordination between creator and taker.
- **Cleaner community** — profanity filtering and moderation‑ready design.

---

## Product Benefits
- **For students:** earn money without leaving campus.
- **For helpers:** discover tasks tailored to your community.
- **For trust:** verification and gated access reduce spam and risk.
- **For speed:** real‑time updates keep quests moving.
